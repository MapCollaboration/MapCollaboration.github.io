Multi-dimensional Analyses of Partonic Distributions

Welcome!

The MAP Collaboration conducts multi-dimensional analyses of partonic distributions through cutting-edge techniques and data-driven approaches. Key objects of study include Transverse Momentum Distributions (TMDs), Generalized Parton Distributions (GPDs), and Fragmentation Functions (FFs).
